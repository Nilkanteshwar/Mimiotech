Refund Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026

This Refund Policy outlines refund handling for Mimiotech Consulting Services. Because most services are customized consulting and recruitment services, refund eligibility is limited and evaluated case-by-case.

General policy

Fees for completed service milestones are generally non-refundable unless otherwise stated in a signed agreement.

When refunds may apply

Refund requests may be considered in case of duplicate payments, billing errors, or non-delivery of agreed paid scope where required documentation is provided.

How to request a refund

Send your request to info@mimiotech.com with payment reference, invoice details, and reason for request. We review requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Processing timeline

Approved refunds are typically processed to the original payment method. Processing time depends on banking and payment providers.

Agreement precedence

If a signed client agreement contains specific billing and refund terms, that agreement takes precedence over this website policy.
